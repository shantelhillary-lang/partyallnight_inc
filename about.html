About PARTYALLNIGHT

PARTYALLNIGHT was founded in 2024 to make finding and booking live events across Kenya simple and reliable.

PARTYALLNIGHT is a leading platform for discovering and booking live music events across Kenya. We connect music lovers with concerts, festivals, and live shows, providing a seamless ticketing experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make live music experiences accessible and enjoyable for everyone. We aim to provide a reliable platform for event organizers and music enthusiasts, ensuring that every concert, festival, and live show is just a click away.

Our Story

PARTYALLNIGHT started as a clothing brand making cool, authentic and extraordinary pieces. It was when we held our first event to launch the clothing brand when we realised how much fun and connection live music could bring to people's lives.The event was a huge success and inspired us to create PARTYALLNIGHT as a platform for music lovers. Our energetic audience gave us the love and support to continue growing into a leading destination for live music experiences across Kenya.
