About ShansiNC

ShansiNC was founded in 2024 to make finding and booking live events across Kenya simple and reliable.

Our Mission

We connect music lovers with the concerts, festivals, and live shows happening across the country, with secure and instant ticket booking.

Our Story

Starting with one small jazz event in Nairobi, ShansiNC has grown to partner with venues and artists across Kenya's coast, highlands, and capital.
